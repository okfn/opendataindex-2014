86 National Statistics
URL: http://www.zimstat.co.zw/


According to the Census and Statistics Act of 2007, Zimbabwean statistics is done by a corporate body - The Zimbabwe National Statistics Agency (ZIMSTAT). The agency suppose to provide quarterly surveys, yet it seems that they can not sustain such reports and that most of the data is out dated. Quarterly reports that do exist are in PDF format and do not give bulk information. There is a link to the African development bank portal, but I couldn't find the information there.
