26 Company Register
URL: http://bizreg.pravosudje.ba/pls/apex/f?p=183:20:1793782965566798


The data seems solely accessible through a web-based search form. It is unclear how often it is updated. It does not seem possible to download the whole registry in bulk. No indication seems to exist on a possible fee to obtain either details or the whole registry, so the data was considered as accessible free of charge as basic information after the web-based query is available for free. The information is not openly licensed as the website seems to be the property of the institution.
